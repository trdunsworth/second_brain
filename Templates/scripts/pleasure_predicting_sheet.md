# Pleasure-Predicting Sheet (Dr. David Burns)

> **Purpose**: Combat anhedonia and depression by scheduling activities, predicting enjoyment, then comparing with actual experience. Based on *Feeling Good* and *Feeling Great*.
> 
> **Instructions**: 
> 1. **Plan** - Schedule 3+ activities per day (mix of mastery + pleasure)
> 2. **Predict** - Rate expected satisfaction 0-100% for each
> 3. **Do** - Complete the activity
> 4. **Record** - Rate actual satisfaction 0-100%
> 5. **Review** - Weekly: compare predictions vs. reality; notice depression's "pessimism bias"
