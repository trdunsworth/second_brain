## Weaknesses Identified

### 1. Content Gaps

- **Limited practical templates** for specific skill development
- **Missing measurement systems** for certain goals (e.g., spiritual growth)
- **Absence of timeline-based roadmaps** for multi-year goals
- **No integration planning** for research-to-practice application

### 2. Structural Limitations

- **Significantly text-heavy** documents
- **Very extensive YAML frontmatter** may overwhelm some users
- **Lack of interactive elements** (notebooks, planners, calendars)
- **No visual organization** tools (mind maps, flow charts)

### 3. User Experience Issues

- **Steep learning curve** for effective system use
- **Requires discipline** to maintain consistent tracking
- **Minimal immediate feedback** mechanisms
- **No engagement features** (community, sharing, challenges)

### 4. Content Quality

- **Dictionary-style Wisdom Source section** lacking practical depth
- **Missing source attribution** details for specific claims
- **No evidence-based approaches** for habit formation
- **Limited customization** for individual learning styles
- **Some wisdom sources lacking practical application guidance**

## Blind Spots

### 1. User Engagement

- **Assumption of high discipline** users
- **No built-in accountability** mechanisms
- **Limited social proof** or community integration
- **No gamification** elements to sustain motivation

### 2. Implementation Reality

- **Ignoring typical life interruptions** and disruptions
- **Overestimating consistent daily tracking** capacity
- **Underestimating time investment** required for comprehensive tracking
- **Not accounting for natural cycles** (bad days, burnout periods)

### 3. Psychological Considerations

- **Lack of trauma-informed** approaches for mental health tracking
- **Not addressing perfectionism** traps in tracking systems
- **Missing self-compassion** frameworks for dealing with missed tracking days
- **No balance between measurement and living** considerations

### 4. Technical Limitations

- **No offline-first design** for maximum accessibility
- **No mobile optimization** for quick updates
- **Limited search functionality** across interconnected documents
- **No analytics for identifying patterns** or bottlenecks
