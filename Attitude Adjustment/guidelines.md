# Attitude Adjustment

## Goal

I want to build a series of documents that will help me adjust my attitude toward the world. However, I don't want to create something that is so stringent that I feel bad if I don't get everything right.

### List of subgoals

- Return to being a good husband
- Improve my patience
- Maintain better focus on projects
- Improve my mood and attitude
- Become a better friend and colleague
- Enhance my leadership and personal skills.
- Enhance my professional skillsets.
- Incorporate my research interests into a program for self-development

## Background

I was diagnosed with Major Depressive Disorder several years ago. Instead of accepting the diagnosis as a starting point to improve my mental health, I wallowed in the diagnosis.

I have started feeling burned out in my professional life. I have allowed that to impact my attitude and I believe that needs to change. To affect that change, I want to build a system that I can use to check myself, re-route myself, and keep myself moving at an even keel, while giving me opportunities for self-improvement.

I have started an LLC with my wife and I want to align my personal work with the LLC's goals of providing reporting, research, and modeling solutions to 911/112/999 centers around the world.

I am a polyglot comfortable in reading multiple languages and want to continue to stretch my skills
