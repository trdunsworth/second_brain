# Attitude Adjustment - Master Plan

## Overview

This is the master plan for the Attitude Adjustment project - a living series of documents and checklists to help merge personal development goals, research interests, and wisdom sources into a comprehensive self-improvement system.

## Goals

The following 8 primary subgoals guide this project:

1. Return to being a good husband
2. Improve my patience
3. Maintain better focus on projects
4. Improve my mood and attitude
5. Become a better friend and colleague
6. Enhance my leadership and personal skills
7. Enhance my professional skillsets
8. Incorporate my research interests into a program for self-development
