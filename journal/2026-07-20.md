## Quick Reference: 10 Cognitive Distortions

| # | Distortion | Brief Definition |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **All-or-Nothing** | Black-and-white; perfect or failure |
| 2 | **Overgeneralization** | Single event = endless pattern ("always/never") |
| 3 | **Mental Filter** | Focus exclusively on negative; ignore positive |
| 4 | **Disqualifying Positive** | Positive experiences "don't count" |
| 5 | **Jumping to Conclusions** | Mind reading (know others' thoughts) / Fortune telling (predict doom) |
| 6 | **Magnification/Minimization** | Blow up negatives; shrink positives |
| 7 | **Emotional Reasoning** | "I feel it → it's true" |
| 8 | **Should Statements** | Rigid rules → guilt (self) / anger (others) |
| 9 | **Labeling** | Global negative label vs. specific behavior |
| 10 | **Personalization** | Blame self for external events not primarily your responsibility |
